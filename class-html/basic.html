Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs of butter
  • 2 tbs of cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium of heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every second (as if you are making an omelette
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
